The golf board game where the dice determine the length of your shot, and you control the direction.

After rolling the dice, choose the destination of the golf ball. You start at the tee and, for each shot, move the ball either straight or diagonally a number of tiles equal to the roll. Different surfaces apply bonuses or penalties that modify the dice roll to determine the final distance. If the adjusted distance is 0 or less, you miss the ball, and it still counts as a shot. Water and forest tiles are invalid and cannot be landed on. The green sourounding the flag also give your the option of a short 1 tile safe shot.

The goal is to reach the golf hole, marked by the flag, in the fewest number of shots.
